Aini Coffee becomes the official supplier of 2016 Kunming Plateau International Half Marathon

(People's Daily Online)    17:26, February 24, 2016

Pu’er Aini Man Zhong Tian Garden Coffee recently became a brand cooperating with the 2016 Kunming Plateau International Half Marathon. Aini Coffee was selected as an official supplier of 2016 Kunming Plateau International Half Marathon. The drip bag coffee of Aini Man Zhong Tian Garden Coffee, as a featured agricultural product of Yunnan Plateau, will be used as one of gifts for the post-game players. With the experience of Kunming Marathon, players can also enjoy the tasty black coffee from Yunnan.

Aini Coffee is a national-level leading business of agricultural industrialization. As a brand representing Yunnan coffee, Aini Coffee has become a joint-venture partner of Starbucks China.
