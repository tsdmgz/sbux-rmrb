30 CEOs from China and US to hold dialogue during Xi's US visit

By Liang Jun (People's Daily Online)    15:56, September 18, 2015
Email|Print
Jack Ma of Alibaba and Tim Cook of Apple

A group of 30 CEOs of some of China's and America's biggest firms will attend a heavyweight business dialogue in Seattle on Sept. 23, which is the first stop of Chinese president Xi Jinping's visit to the U.S. between Sept. 22 and Sept. 25, Henry M. Paulson Jr announced on Sept. 16 (local time). 

Xi will be present at a roundtable hosted by Paulson, the former U.S. Treasury secretary, and deliver a speech. The attendees include 15 CEOs from China and 15 from the U.S.

The dialogue is co-sponsored by Paulson Foundation and the China International Trade Promotion Committee. It will provide opportunity to the Sino-US business leaders to discuss issues facing the two countries, challenges and opportunities, and to explore ways strengthening the Sino-US economic and trade relations.

President Xi will tour Microsoft Co. and Boeing Corp on the first leg of the U.S. visit.
