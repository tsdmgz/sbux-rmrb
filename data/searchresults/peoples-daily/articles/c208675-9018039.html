Aini Man Zhong Tian Garden is selected as one of the top ten new landmarks of featured tour of Yunnan

(People's Daily Online)    17:14, February 15, 2016

On January 30, Aini Man Zhong Tian Garden in Pu’er was awarded the honorable title of “Top Ten New Landmarks of Featured Tour of Yunnan”, becoming the only coffee garden selected in the coffee industry of Yunnan.

Aini Man Zhong Tian Garden is located in Bangnong Village Committee, Yixiang Town, Simao District, Pu’er City, Yunnan. Situated in the “Gold Belt for Planting Coffee” of Pu’er as the “City of Chinese Coffee”, Aini Man Zhong Tian Garden is the site of 30,000-mu pasture and rainforest coffee garden of Yunnan Aini Group, which is the joint venture partner of Starbucks China and the national-level leading enterprise in agricultural industrialization.
